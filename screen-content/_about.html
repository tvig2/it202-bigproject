About our origin.

Starting in early January 2019, we came together and wanted to figure out a way to connect landlords and tenants together as a middleman. That's when we came up with Zoom.

This application was developed by Tarush Vig, a computer science student at the University of Illinois at Chicago. In conjunction with a few other students from UIC, this idea was brought to life through time and effort put in by everyone.

We hope you enjoy your apartment search!
